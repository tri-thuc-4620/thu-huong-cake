Buoc 1. Chon mau banh va kich thuoc banh

Tai Thu Huong Cake, ban se co rat nhieu su lua chon ve kieu dang va kich thuoc cua chiec banh kem. De lua chon duoc chiec banh kem phu hop, vua can doi duoc chi phi vua tranh lang phi banh, ban nen xem xet ve nhu cau va quy mo cua bua tiec.

Sau day, Thu Huong xin gui bang kich thuoc banh kem va khau phan an tuong ung de ban co the thuan tien tham khao.

Buoc 2. Lua chon cot banh (Phan banh gato ben trong)

Thu Huong Cake cung cap nhieu loai cot banh khac nhau de ban lua chon:

  • Gato Vani Nhan Viet Quat - Vi ngot thanh, chua nhe cua viet quat
  • Gato Red Velvet - Vi dac trung cua Red Velvet mem min
  • Gato Socola - Vi dang nhe, thom nong cua socola
  • Gato Tra Xanh - Vi thanh mat cua tra xanh Nhat Ban
  • Gato Dau Tay - Vi ngot tu nhien cua dau tay tuoi

Buoc 3. Hoan thien don banh va gui Don Dat Banh

3.1 Dien cac thong tin can thiet bao gom:

  • Ho va ten nguoi dat
  • So dien thoai lien he
  • Thoi gian nhan banh
  • Dia chi giao hang (neu chon giao tan noi)
  • Ghi chu don hang (loi chuc len banh, yeu cau dac biet...)

3.2 Kiem tra lai don hang va lua chon phuong thuc thanh toan:

Sau khi dien day du thong tin, ban kiem tra lai don hang mot lan nua de dam bao thong tin chinh xac. Sau do lua chon phuong thuc thanh toan phu hop:

  • Chuyen khoan ngan hang - Quet ma QR de thanh toan nhanh chong
  • Thanh toan khi nhan hang (COD) - Thanh toan tien mat cho shipper

3.3 Tien hanh thanh toan:

Neu lua chon phuong thuc chuyen khoan ngan hang, ban chi can quet ma QR hoac chuyen khoan theo thong tin tai khoan duoc hien thi. Don hang se duoc xac nhan ngay sau khi nhan duoc thanh toan.
